Mission

Our mission is to be a leading international consultation team in charge of offering quality sports services and excellence. In addition to promoting and disseminating the practice of professional football on all continents; that together with the work team is to provide and guarantee athletes a professional soccer success. To achieve our mission GKU Intl. LLC will be committed and joining efforts day by day to reach those we want.
• The professionalization of sports athletes of an amateur category at a professional level.
• To be a comprehensive consultancy that has a different emphasis, such as emotional and mental support that allows the athlete to be supported in sports practice with optimal and transversal results.
• Achieve a balance in physical, tactical and technical work, as a fundamental part to achieve excellence in sports performance and proper professionalization.



Vision

Our vision is particularly focused on our athletes; which is to have a wide portfolio of soccer players, with desire and passion for this sport; Have the strength, dedication and discipline to achieve professionalization in the best known sport worldwide.
Develop a wide portfolio of athletes who professionalize anywhere in the world, and thanks to our extensive network of connection, we offer excellent alternatives for our clients and partners, providing high levels of quality in the international market.
